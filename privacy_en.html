Privacy Policy

Last updated: 05 September 2025 • Authorized domain: ai-helper-bot.ru

1. Introduction

Thank you for using AI Google Disk Helper Bot (the “Service”). This Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, how long we keep it, how we share it, and what choices and rights you have. It applies when you interact with the bot on Telegram and connect your Google Drive via OAuth 2.0.

The Service’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. Controller & Contact

Controller: AI Helper Team
Contact: ai.bot.helper.email@gmail.com
Platform: Telegram bot “AI_Google_Disk_helper_bot”.

3. Applicability & Roles

We act as controller for the processing described here. Telegram remains an independent platform that processes your messages under its own terms and policy. Google acts as a separate provider of your Google Account and Google Drive.

4. Data We Collect

4.1 Google Account Data

  • Email and basic profile (userinfo.email, userinfo.profile), and OpenID identifiers (openid).

4.2 Google Drive Data

We access only files/folders you explicitly select:

  • Metadata: file ID, name, MIME type, size, modified time.
  • Content: extracted text from supported formats (PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV, XLSX).

4.3 Telegram Data

  • Telegram user ID (chat ID), messages/commands you send to the bot, and files you upload in chat.

4.4 Authentication Tokens

  • OAuth 2.0 access/refresh tokens and expiry to maintain your connection.

4.5 Logs & Diagnostics

  • Timestamps, request/response status, error traces, and aggregated performance metrics.

4.6 Data We Ask You Not to Provide

Please do not upload or connect files containing special categories of data (e.g., health, biometric, precise geolocation, government IDs, financial account/PCI data, or other regulated/sensitive content). If you do, you are responsible for ensuring a lawful basis and all required notices/consents.

5. Google OAuth Scopes Requested

We request the narrowest scopes needed to provide the Service:

  • openid, https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email, https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.profile — identify you during OAuth.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.metadata.readonly — list metadata of files you selected and keep the index in sync.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly — read the content of the files you selected to answer your questions.

We do not request broad write or delete scopes. If the technical implementation changes, this page and the OAuth consent screen will be updated accordingly before any new scopes are requested.

6. Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — you grant Google OAuth consent to connect your Drive.
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — provide the core features you ask for (Q&A over your selected files).
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — security, abuse prevention, debugging, and service improvement with minimal impact on your privacy.

7. Purposes of Processing

  1. Service delivery — extract text from selected files and generate answers in chat.
  2. Personalization — remember necessary context to answer follow-ups.
  3. Security — verify identity, rate-limit abuse, and ensure operational integrity.
  4. Diagnostics — monitor performance and fix errors.

We do not sell data, build advertising profiles, or use your data for ad targeting.

8. Limited Use Compliance

  • Data is used only to provide or improve user-facing features described here.
  • No human access to your content unless you explicitly request support by email, for security/abuse investigation, or where required by law.
  • No transfer to third parties except to provide the user-facing features, for security/legal reasons, or as part of a lawful corporate transaction with notice and equivalent protections.
