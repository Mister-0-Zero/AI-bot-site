AI Google Disk Helper Bot

Home page • Authorized domain: ai-helper-bot.ru

AI-bot is a Telegram assistant that connects to your Google Drive only with your explicit consent and answers questions about the content of the files you select (TXT, PDF, DOCX, CSV, XLSX). It extracts text from your documents and generates answers in chat.

Open in Telegram Privacy Policy Terms of Service

How it works

  1. Start the bot in Telegram and tap “Connect Google Drive”.
  2. Google shows the official OAuth consent screen (English). You approve or deny.
  3. The bot reads only the files/folders you choose and builds a private text index.
  4. Ask questions in chat; the bot answers grounded in your own content.

You can revoke access anytime at Google Account → Security → Third-party access.

Key features

  • Granular file/folder selection from Google Drive.
  • Accurate answers grounded in your documents.
  • Context-aware conversation and follow-ups.
  • Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV, Excel.

Google scopes used & justification

  • openid, userinfo.email, userinfo.profile — identify the user during OAuth.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.metadata.readonly — list selected files and keep the index in sync.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly — read the content of the files you selected to answer your questions.

We request only the minimum necessary scopes. The app does not ask for broad write access.

Use of Google user data

  • Used solely to provide the features above (Q&A over your selected files).
  • No selling or sharing with advertisers/data brokers.
  • No human review unless you explicitly ask for support by email.

Limited Use: The app’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data retention & deletion

  • We store minimal metadata and a derived text index from the files you selected.
  • You can request deletion of your index and metadata at any time: ai.bot.helper.email@gmail.com.
  • Revoking access immediately stops any further reading of your Drive.
