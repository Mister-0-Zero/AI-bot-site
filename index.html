AI Google Disk Helper Bot

AI‑bot is an intelligent Telegram assistant that connects to your Google Drive, extracts text from documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT, XLSX, CSV) and answers questions using a large language model (LLM).

How It Works

  • You sign in with Google (OAuth 2.0)
  • The bot gains access only to the files or folders you select
  • The Service indexes text and builds a private knowledge base
  • You ask questions in Telegram — the bot responds based on your documents

Key Features

  • Accurate answers grounded in your files
  • Strategy, ideas, analytics, and recommendations
  • Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV, Excel
  • Context‑aware conversation and follow‑up questions
  • Granular file/folder selection from Google Drive

OAuth Scopes Requested

We request the narrowest scopes needed:

  • userinfo.email / userinfo.profile — identify the user
  • openid — secure authentication
  • drive.metadata.readonly — view file metadata
  • drive.readonly — read the content of selected files
  • drive.file — restrict access to items chosen in the picker

Security

Your data is never used for advertising or analytics. Content is encrypted; access tokens are tied to your Telegram ID. Revoke access anytime in your Google account settings:

myaccount.google.com/permissions
